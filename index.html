Dialouge

Keigo: Its quiet out here,

Kotone: -use any profanity language here and dropping the cup of coffee-

Keigo: I'm sorry, I didn't mean to scare you-

Kotone: Oh, no, you're fine -pause and rub your hand- you just startled me. Jesus why don't your wings make any sound when you come swooping in? At least give a gal a warning.

Keigo: -smiles- I wanted to catch you before I took off -tend to her wounds- You left your thermos on the counter.

Kotone: -smiles with a small laugh- You'd risk being seen with me just to bring me my coffee?

Keigo: And to get my goodbye, since you ran off without one. I was worried!

Kotone: -grab the thermo from Keigo- Thank you very much, the only reason why I do that is because you never get any sleep - I was trying to be nice! And you know when I leave for work, Birdbrain. I even left you a note on the fridge

Keigo: Ah, I didn't see it, -scratch the back of your head in embarrassment- Sorry....

Kotone: -smile- Don't be. I'm glad you came. Waiting out here is the crummiest part of the day, usually. Company is nice, especially if its yours.
